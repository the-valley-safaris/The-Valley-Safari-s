Hunting & Safari

Can I bring my own firearm?
Yes. South Africa allows temporary importation of hunting rifles. We assist all hunters with SAPS Form 520 and will meet you at the airport. Alternatively, rental rifles are available at the lodge.
What animals can I hunt?
We offer plains game such as kudu, impala, warthog, wildebeest, and more. Custom packages are available based on species and duration.
Do I need hunting experience?
No prior experience is necessary. Our PH (Tihard Strydom) will guide you every step of the way. We cater for seasoned hunters and first-timers alike.
